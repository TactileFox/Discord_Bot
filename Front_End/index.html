Discord Bot: Main Page

Welcome to the front end for my Discord bot!


Features

Monitor Server Health

Stay on top of your server's activity with real-time monitoring tools. Get insights into your server's health, performance, and usage trends to ensure everything is running smoothly.

Maximize Engagement

Unlock the power of timing to boost engagement! Analyze user activity patterns to schedule events, announcements, and interactions when your community is most active.

User Insights

Gain a deeper understanding of your most active and influential users. Use data-driven insights to tailor your engagement strategies and recognize your community's power users.

Astronomy

Embark on an astronomical adventure with the /get_astronomy command. Explore distant planets, constellations, and solar systems directly from your Discord server.

Weather

Stay updated with the latest weather forecasts for any US city using /get_weather! Whether you're planning an event or just curious, get accurate and timely weather information at your fingertips.

Snipe

Ever had a user delete a risky message right after posting? With /snipe, you can catch and preserve those messages, ensuring nothing is lost in the chat history.

Welcome to the server health tab! Filter to a specific server or all of them!
